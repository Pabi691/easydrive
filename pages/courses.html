Course FAQs

Do I need to pass my theory before booking?

Not at all! You can book a course that includes a fast-tracked theory test. You must pass it before your practical test takes place.

Can I do the course over weekends?

Yes, many of our instructors offer weekend flexibility. During booking, let us know your availability and we will match you accordingly.

What happens if I need more hours?

If your instructor feels you need a few extra hours before the test, these can be purchased directly through the instructor at their standard hourly rate.
