About Easy-Drive.UK

We believe learning to drive shouldn't take years. Discover our mission to get you on the road safely and quickly.

Our Story & Mission

Easy-Drive.UK was founded out of frustration with the traditional, painfully slow method of learning to drive. Waiting months for lessons and even longer for practical tests shouldn't be the norm.

We designed a completely modern intensive course system that does the heavy lifting for you. We match you with top-tier, DVSA-approved local instructors and automatically fast-track your practical test, cutting out the waiting queues entirely. Our mission is to transform weeks and months of waiting into a clear, focused path to your driving licence.

Why Choose Us?

  • Fast-Track Test Booking

    We use dedicated software to monitor DVSA cancellations, booking your test months before standard wait times.

  • DVSA Approved Instructors

    Only strict, highly-rated local instructors who know your specific test routes.

  • PassProtect Guarantee

    Failing happens. We give you a second (and third) chance for free if things go wrong on the day.
